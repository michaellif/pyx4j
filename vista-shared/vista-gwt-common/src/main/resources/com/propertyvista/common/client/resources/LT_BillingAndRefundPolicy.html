Billing

The billing cycle and amount is based on your lease agreement. We follow all applicable local laws. If you have signed up for Pre-Authorized Payments, we will automatically debit your account based on the agreement you have signed. For more information around your amount owing and legal responsibilities, please refer to your lease agreement.

Notice to Vacate

Please refer to your lease agreement for the local rules and regulation around the notice time period to vacate your apartment to avoid any penalties. Notices must comply to your lease agreement and need to be submitted according to that agreement.
