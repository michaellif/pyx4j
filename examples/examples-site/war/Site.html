Welcome to Curious Kids Daycare Center - a safe, fun and nurturing place for kids from 15 months to 5 years of age. Curious Kids is a place where children learn, grow and develop their natural curiosity through play and interaction with peers and qualified staff members.

 Curious Kids is happy to inform all parents that the Centre will be engaging in a pre-school in depth preparation of the French language. Our unique pedagogical approach will allow children of all ages to learn French through a combination of play, music and other recreational activities. For parents who are looking at enrolling their children in French-immersion schools, this will provide an excellent opportunity at exposing their children to French at their earliest stages of development.

 After School Program for School Age Children is now open!

  • Hot meals and snacks for your child.
  • Homework assistance.
  • Extra-curricular age appropriate activities.
  • Fun and relaxing after-school environment.

Location:

Our bright and newly reconstructed facility is located on a quiet and calm street in the centre of downtown Richmond Hill. Our facility is easily accessible to private vehicles and the public transportation network. Our centre is close to the Richmond Hill Station.

Facility:

Our building is surrounded by a beautiful, green backyard resembling a stunning countryside. Our premise contains two large rooms for children's activities, staff room, an office, kitchen and a large gymnasium. Our spacious playrooms are equipped with new age-appropriate equipment. Moreover, our newly built gymnasium contains safe equipment, which allows us to incorporate physical education programs for kids as part of our daily routine.

Staff:

Our staff consists of Ontario Certified Early Childhood Educators with years of experience in Ontario Daycares. Our qualified and caring teachers and teacher assistants all hold current CPR/First Aid Certificates.

Nutrition:

Curious Kids takes pride in the healthy food that is prepared daily for our children. We believe that healthy nutrition is essential for developing young minds. We educate our children and their parents of the importance of healthy eating. We offer 3 daily hot meals with a variety of choices, all of which follow the guidelines recommended by the Ontario Ministry of Health.

Toddlers:

(15 - 30 months - Staff Ratio 5:1)

Our unique full-day program offers a safe environment for toddlers, granting the opportunity to develop the children's autonomous thinking and a sense of self as separate and unique individuals. Our well-designed program runs in a secure manner and meets the children's needs through specific routines based on careful attention to individual differences in preferences and habits. We realize that toddlers are creatures on the move. Their drive to explore combined with short attention spans means that toddlers learn as they go. Therefore our staff is always ready to prepare interesting physical and learning environments for our toddlers. At this age a lot of emphasis is put on individual activities. Our qualified staff reinforces toddler language development through modeling, books, children songs and finger play. We make sure that by the end of the toddler program and before moving on to the preschool level all of our kids are fully toilet trained.

Preschoolers:

(30 months to 5 years - Staff Ratio 8:1)

The daily curricula, as designed for preschoolers, include activities that are meant to stimulate curious young minds in training. We believe that preschoolers need constant interaction with peers and adults as well as a variety of individual activities. As preschoolers' language skills are still developing, our teachers integrate the use of language and language materials into active play. Children are encouraged to develop oral language and listening skills through conversations with playmates and adults. We also offer materials that help our children practice writing skills and language comprehension. Our qualified staff members plan group activities that involve children in all aspects of communication. Other than the important language development activities, to which much attention is provided, our preschoolers participate in a variety of workshops that include visual arts, music, drama, science and physical education. Children take part in the preparation of various holiday and thematic celebrations, where they can share what they have learned at the centre with parents, siblings and other family members. Our preschool program also includes a wide variety of extra-curricular activities in such fields as tennis, chess, visual arts, dance, drama, foreign languages, piano lessons and Yoga for kids. Our rich preschool program fully prepares our children for Grade One and we take pride in our graduates.
