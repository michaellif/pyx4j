If the Landlord is unable to provide possession of the Premises at the commencement of this Tenancy Agreement because a previous Tenant refused to leave, or for any other reason, the Landlord will not be responsible for failure to give the Tenant possession on that date; however, the Rent payable under this Tenancy Agreement will be adjusted to be payable from the date vacant possession of the Premises is provided. The term of this Tenancy Agreement will not be extended but if the Premises is not provided to the Tenant within thirty (30) days from the commencement of this Agreement, either party may terminate this Agreement by giving the other notice in writing.
