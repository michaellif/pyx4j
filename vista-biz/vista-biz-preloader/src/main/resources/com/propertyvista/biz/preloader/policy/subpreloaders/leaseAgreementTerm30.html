a. The Tenant shall not alter or add to the locking system on any door giving direct entry to the Premises or storage locker. In the event that the Tenant does alter or add to the locking system on any door giving direct entry to the Premises or storage locker, the Tenant will be responsible to pay to the Landlord the designated fee.

 

b. The Tenant consents to any change of the locks in the building by the Landlord including the door giving direct entry into the Premises provided the Landlord gives the Tenant replacement keys.

 

c. The Tenant shall reimburse the Landlord for any replacement keys required by the Tenant.

 

d. In the event the Tenant or his guest(s) locks himself out of the Premises or the Residential Complex, the Tenant shall be responsible for all costs of re-entry including, but not limited to, locksmith charges, charges for damages howsoever caused, and any service charge payable to the Landlord if the Landlord agrees to unlock the Premises or the Residential Complex.

 

e. For the purpose of this lease, “key” includes any device used to gain access to locked entry.
