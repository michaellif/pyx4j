a. Agreement

      i.        The parties to this Residential Tenancy Agreement (hereinafter referred to as "this Agreement") agree to be legally bound by and comply with the terms of this Agreement. The parties understand that where in this Agreement the words "the Act" are used, they refer to Residential Tenancies legislation applicable in the province in which the Rented Premises are located .

     ii.        It is agreed that the Landlord shall be entitled to enforce the provisions of this Agreement against the Tenant in a Court or Tribunal of competent jurisdiction, in the event of a breach thereof by the Tenant and the Landlord shall have the right to re-enter the Premises and obtain vacant possession of the Premises in the event of non-payment of rent or breach of any of the provisions of this Agreement by the Tenant.

    iii.        The provisions hereof shall be read with all grammatical and gender changes necessary and any singular reference to the Tenant shall be deemed to include all Tenants to this Agreement.

 

b. Definitions

      i.        "Tenant" refers to all Tenants named as such in this Tenancy Agreement and, in the Province of Ontario, any person who is a Tenant by claiming or obtaining such status by virtue of being a spouse of a Tenant named as such in this Tenancy Agreement.

     ii.        "Landlord" refers to TransGlobe Property Management Services, its authorized employees, agents, consultants,  representatives, successors and assigns.

    iii.        "Parties" refers to the Landlord and the Tenant.

    iv.        "Premises" means, the rental unit described in clause 3 and the right to use all common areas at the Residential Complex for all usual purposes subject to the terms of this agreement.
