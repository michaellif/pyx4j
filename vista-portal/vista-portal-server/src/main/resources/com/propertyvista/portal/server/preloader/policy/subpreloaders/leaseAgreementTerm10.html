a. If the Monthly Rent is paid by cheque and the cheque is not honoured and returned due to insufficient funds or any other reason by the bank upon which it is drawn, the Tenant shall pay to the Landlord, in respect of the dishonoured cheque, an amount to reimburse the Landlord for NSF and other bank charges levied against the Landlord for each such cheque in addition to the aforementioned Monthly Rent.

 

b.

      i.        British Columbia: Late payment, returned or non-sufficient funds (N.S.F.) cheques, are subject to an administrative fee of not more than $25.00 each, plus the amount of any service fees charged by a financial institution to the Landlord. Although these fees are payable by the Tenant to the Landlord, the failure to pay the rent on the due date is a breach of a material term of this Agreement. The obligation of the Tenant under this Agreement and by law requires the rent to be paid on the date this it is due.

     ii.        All Other Provinces: Arrears of rent shall bear interest at the rate of 6% per annum calculated from the date following the date upon which the rent is due until paid and such interest shall be deemed as rent hereunder, subject to any limitation that may apply pursuant provincial legislation

    iii.        The Tenant hereby acknowledges and agrees that any late payment of rent may be recorded at the discretion of the Landlord with any credit agency or credit bureau and may be used by the Landlord in support of an application to terminate this agreement. Permission is hereby granted to the Landlord to obtain a consumer report in the event the Tenant is in arrears of rent or wishes to renew this Tenancy Agreement.

 

c. In the event of the bankruptcy of the Tenant, the Landlord shall rank as a preferred creditor pursuant to the Bankruptcy and Insolvency Act in respect of arrears of rent for a period of three months next preceding the bankruptcy. If the Tenant becomes a bankrupt, the balance of the term of this Tenancy Agreement shall be terminated and the Tenant shall become a month to month Tenant subject to all of the terms and conditions of this Tenancy Agreement and subject to the rights of the Trustee.
