All garbage shall be wrapped in plastic or disposable garbage bags and tied and sorted for recycling purposes, if required, and placed in the areas designated by the Landlord and at such times which it may designate, all in conformity with health regulations and any applicable recycling and municipal waste disposal regulations. The Tenant shall indemnify the Landlord for any damages or any other liability which may accrue at law to the Landlord because of the Tenant’s breach of this provision.

 

b. The Tenant shall not leave garbage in the chute room or other common areas. The Tenant will be liable to the Landlord for the cost of having the garbage removed and the area cleaned.

 

c. The Tenant shall not bring or permit the bringing or storage of any contaminants or noxious, dangerous or toxic substances in or upon the Premises. The Tenant shall indemnify the Landlord for any damages or any other liability which may accrue at law to the Landlord because of the Tenant’s breach of this provision. If a question arises as to whether a substance or thing is a contaminant or a noxious, dangerous or toxic substance or thing, such question will be determined having regard to provincial or federal law or by a person whom the Landlord believes to be an expert qualified to determine the question and the Tenant shall be responsible for the cost of determining same.
