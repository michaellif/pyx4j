a. Assignment

      i.        The Tenant will not assign the tenancy herein without first requesting, in writing, and receiving written leave of the Landlord to do so.

     ii.        The Tenant acknowledges the right of the Landlord to consent or refuse to consent to the assignment of this tenancy.

    iii.        If the Landlord consents to an assignment of this tenancy, the Tenant shall not assign the Premises to a potential assignee without first requesting, in writing, that the Landlord consent to the assignment of the Premises to the potential assignee and receiving the Landlord’s written consent thereto, which consent will not be arbitrarily or unreasonably refused. In the event the Tenant requests the Landlord to consent to an assignment or sublet of this tenancy agreement, the Tenant agrees to pay the Landlord an administration and processing fee, the amount of which shall be determined solely by the Landlord in accordance with applicable law, provided that this provision shall not be construed to mean acceptance by the Landlord of a prospective assignee.

    iv.        It is further agreed that a request to assign shall be deemed not to have been made until the administration and processing fee has been paid and submitted along with the Tenant’s written request. Each written request made under this section shall be delivered, personally or by mail, to the Landlord at the address set out on page 1 of this Agreement and where the request is mailed, it shall be deemed to have been made on the 5th day after mailing.

     v.        No assignment shall occur until the Tenant and assignee have completed all documentation reasonably required by the Landlord in respect of the assignment.

    vi.        Until the assignment occurs, the Tenant shall remain liable for all obligations under this Agreement, including the obligation to pay rent. Unless otherwise agreed in writing, the Tenant shall be responsible for finding an acceptable assignee provided the Landlord has first agreed, in writing, to the assignment of the tenancy.

 

b. Sublet

      i.        The Tenant covenants not to sublet the Premises without first requesting, in writing, and obtaining the Landlord’s written consent to sublet to the prospective sub-tenant which consent will not be arbitrarily or unreasonably refused.

     ii.        Where the tenancy is monthly, the Tenant shall not sublet the Premises for a term greater than the remaining days in the month when the sublet takes effect.

    iii.        Where the tenancy is for a fixed term, the Tenant shall not sublet the Premises for a term which ends after the fixed term.

    iv.        The Tenant and sub-tenant shall complete documentation, reasonably required by the Landlord in respect of the sublet prior to commencement of the sub-tenancy. In all cases, no consent to a sublet shall be granted by the Landlord unless the Tenant first provides to the Landlord a copy of a signed, written sub-tenancy agreement specifying the date of termination of the subtenancy and specifying that the sub-tenancy agreement shall not be amended by the parties without first obtaining the written consent of the Landlord thereto, which consent may be unreasonably withheld.

     v.        In the event the Tenant requests the Landlord to consent to a sublet of this tenancy, the Tenant agrees to pay the Landlord an administration and processing fee, the amount of which shall be determined solely by the Landlord in accordance with applicable law, provided that this provision shall not be construed to mean acceptance by the Landlord of a sublet.

    vi.        It is further deemed that a request to sublet shall not have been made until the administration and processing fee has been paid.

   vii.        The Tenant acknowledges that, in the event the Premises is sublet, the Tenant shall continue to be bound by all of the provisions of this Tenancy Agreement, including the obligation to pay rent, until such time as the tenancy is terminated. The Tenant further acknowledges that any sub-tenant must vacate the Premises and the Tenant must deliver vacant possession of the Premises upon the termination of this Tenancy Agreement.
