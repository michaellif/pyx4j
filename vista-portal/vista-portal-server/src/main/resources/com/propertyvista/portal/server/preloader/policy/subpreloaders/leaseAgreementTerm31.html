The Tenant agrees that the Landlord may enter the Premises:

      i.        in order to make maintenance inspections, repairs and renovations, including implementing pest control measures, regardless of whether the Tenant believes such inspections, repairs, renovations or measures are required, and the Landlord shall not be treated as a trespasser and does not constitute a breach of any express or implied covenant of reasonable enjoyment for the purpose of such entry; furthermore, the Tenant agrees that the Landlord may enter the Premises in the manner and for all purposes permitted under any applicable legislation. It is further agreed that the Landlord’s exercise of rights under this clause shall not be a breach of the covenant with the Tenant for quiet enjoyment of the Premises.

     ii.        upon 24 hours written notice, which states the purpose for entering and which must be reasonable, and the date and the time of the entry, which must be between times as governed by provincial legislation unless the Tenant agrees otherwise, and the Tenant agrees that it is good and sufficient notice for the Landlord to specify a range of hours such as “between 8:00 a.m. and 6:00 p.m.” in specifying the proposed time of entry,;

    iii.        without notice, if there is an emergency and the entry is necessary to protect life or property;

    iv.        if either party has given notice of termination of this Agreement or if the parties have agreed that the tenancy will be terminated, the Premises may be shown to prospective Tenants in accordance with provincial legislation, as amended from time to time.

     v.        if the Tenant gives the Landlord permission to enter at the time of entry or not more than 30 days before the entry;

    vi.        if the Tenant has abandoned the Premises;

   vii.        pursuant to an order of an arbitrator, tribunal or court saying the Landlord may enter the Premises;

  viii.        if the Landlord is providing housekeeping or related services and the entry is for that purpose and at a reasonable time.
