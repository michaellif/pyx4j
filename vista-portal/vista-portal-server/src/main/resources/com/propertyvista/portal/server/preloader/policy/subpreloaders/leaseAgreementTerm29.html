a. The Tenant will:

      i.        During the entire period of this tenancy and any renewal thereof, at his sole cost and expense, obtain and keep in full force and effect, fire, flood and property damage and public liability insurance in an amount equal to that which the Landlord, acting reasonably, considers adequate. The Tenant agrees to provide to the Landlord, prior to taking possession of the Premises and thereafter upon demand at any time, proof that all such insurance is in effect and the Tenant shall notify the Landlord in writing if such insurance is cancelled or otherwise terminated.

     ii.        indemnify the Landlord and save it harmless from and against any and all claims, actions, damages, liability and expenses in connection with loss of life, personal injury and/or damage to property arising from any occurrence in the Premises and the Residential Complex, the use thereof by the Tenant, or occasioned wholly or in part by any act or omission of the Tenant, or by anyone permitted to be in the Premises or the Residential Complex by the Tenant;

    iii.        not cause or permit damage to the Premises or the Residential Complex;

    iv.        be liable for any damage resulting from water left running from taps in the Premises;

     v.        not do anything or omit to do anything in the Premises or the Residential Complex which impairs the safety of other lawful rights, privilege or interest of any other Tenant or the Landlord; and

    vi.        where applicable, remove snow from the driveway, sidewalk, entranceway, patio, balcony, stairs and outside designated parking space

 

b. The Landlord shall not in any event whatsoever be liable or responsible in any way for:

      i.        any personal injury or death that may be suffered or sustained by the Tenant, an occupant, or any member of the Tenants’ family, his agents or guests, or any other person who may be upon the Premises or the Residential Complex; or

     ii.        any loss of or damage or injury to any property including, but not limited to, cars and contents thereof belonging to the Tenant or to any member of the Tenants’ family or to any other person while such property is on the Premises or on the Residential Complex; or

    iii.        without limiting the generality of the foregoing, any damages to any such property caused by steam, water, ground water, rain or snow which may leak into, issue or flow from any part of the Premises or the Residential Complex or from the water, steam, sprinkler or drainage pipes or plumbing works of the same or from any place or quarter; or

    iv.        any damage caused by or attributable to the condition or arrangement of any electrical or other wiring; or

     v.        any damage caused by anything done or omitted to be done by any Tenants of the Landlord, including, without limiting the generality of the foregoing, damage or injury caused by the presence of moulds or related organisms in the Premises; or

    vi.        any damage to or loss of any property left in or on the Premises or the Residential Complex subsequent to the Tenant giving up possession of the Premises whether or not said delivery of possession by the Tenant was voluntary, whether caused or attributable to anything done or omitted to be done by the Landlord or any other Tenants of the Landlord, or any other person; or

   vii.        any damage to or loss of property incurred by the Tenant as a result of an “Act of God”, being such as but not limited to, the following: severe storm, lightning, flood, infestation of vermin or insects, etc.

 

c. Except where the Premises are rendered unfit for the purposes of the Tenant as a result of an “Act of God” or the negligence of the Landlord, the Tenant shall be liable for full payment of rent for the Premises and shall be liable to reimburse and indemnify the Landlord in respect of payments made or liable to be made by the Landlord to any insurer or to any other person in respect of lost income and damages of any kind and shall be liable to pay the full Monthly Rent during the period when the Premises are unfit for the purposes of the Tenant.

 

d. In the event of damage, destruction or disposition of the Tenants’ property, which the Tenant believes has resulted from an act or omission of the Landlord, Landlords’ Agent(s) or Building Representative(s), the Tenant agrees to notify the Landlord on the next business day, in writing, of such damage, destruction or disposition and to provide written particulars of same, including the alleged cause. The Tenant further agrees not to dispose of, repair or replace any such property without first permitting the Landlord to inspect it. In addition, where the Tenant proposes to incur expenses which the Tenant believes have resulted from an act or omission of the Landlord, Landlords’ Agent(s) or Building Representative(s), the Tenant shall first notify the Landlord of the intention to incur such expenses and shall permit the Landlord an opportunity to propose other arrangements which may be more effective and less costly than those for which the Tenant proposes to incur expense. In all cases of damage to property, however caused, the Tenant shall notify his insurer of the damage and shall file a claim with his insurer for the full amount of the loss.
